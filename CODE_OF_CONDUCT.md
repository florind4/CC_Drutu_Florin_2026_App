# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and discuss ideas, not people.
- Give actionable feedback.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Doxxing or sharing private information.
- Any behavior that makes collaboration unsafe.

## Reporting

Report issues privately to the maintainers via the security/support channels described in `SECURITY.md` and `SUPPORT.md`.
